Mass Shooting Data Analysis in the United States, 2018-2022

Downloading the dataset from Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hemil26/mass-shootings-in-united-states-20182022?resource=download), I received data on mass shootings in the United States from 2018 to 2022. I analyzed the data using pandas and python.
